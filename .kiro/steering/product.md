# Product Overview

## AI-Powered Project Portfolio Management (PPM) Platform

This is an intelligent SaaS alternative to Cora that combines traditional portfolio management with advanced AI capabilities. The platform serves portfolio managers, resource managers, and executives with comprehensive project oversight and intelligent decision-making support.

## Core Value Proposition

- **AI-Enhanced Decision Making**: Resource optimization, risk forecasting, and intelligent reporting through specialized AI agents
- **Comprehensive Portfolio Management**: Full project lifecycle management with real-time dashboards and analytics
- **Natural Language Reporting**: RAG-based system for generating custom reports from natural language queries
- **Intelligent Automation**: Automated workflows, risk detection, and resource allocation suggestions

## Key Features

### Portfolio Management
- Real-time project dashboards with health indicators (green/yellow/red)
- Portfolio-level metrics aggregation and KPI tracking
- Project lifecycle management from planning to completion

### AI-Powered Capabilities
- **Resource Optimizer Agent**: Analyzes allocations and suggests optimizations based on skills, availability, and workload
- **Risk Forecaster Agent**: Predicts project risks using historical patterns and current indicators
- **RAG Reporter**: Generates custom reports from natural language queries
- **Hallucination Validator**: Ensures AI-generated content accuracy

### Financial & Risk Management
- Multi-currency budget tracking with real-time variance analysis
- Centralized risk and issue registers with automated linking
- Configurable approval workflows with parallel/sequential patterns

## Target Users

- **Portfolio Managers**: Dashboard oversight, project health monitoring, strategic decision making
- **Resource Managers**: Team allocation, capacity planning, skill matching
- **Project Managers**: Risk management, issue tracking, milestone monitoring
- **Executives**: High-level reporting, financial oversight, strategic insights
- **Financial Controllers**: Budget monitoring, cost analysis, financial reporting

## Success Metrics

- Portfolio performance optimization through AI recommendations
- Reduced project risks through predictive forecasting
- Improved resource utilization and allocation efficiency
- Faster decision-making through natural language reporting
